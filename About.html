About Me

Debaditya Majumder

I am a graduate of Don Bosco School, Liluah, having completed my higher secondary education in 2025. I was honoured to serve as the co-valedictorian of my batch in both the Indian Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) in 2023 and the Indian School Certificate (ISC) examinations in 2025.

In my schooling years, I dabbled in various extracurricular activities that piqued my interest spanning from coding and quizzing to acting and scriptwriting. In the academic year 2024–2025, I served as the President of the Literary Club, where I collaborated closely with our Club Animators to organize events, manage finances, and maintain a detailed record of our initiatives.

To further hone my leadership and organizational skills, I took on the role of Student Coordinator for our school’s Yearbook during the academic year 2023–2024. After eight months of dedicated work, our team successfully launched a Cosmos-themed Yearbook featuring creative innovations over the previous edition, with the aim of inspiring students to explore and contribute to the literary arts.

I did a Physics project on atmospheric stability and lapse rate applying core principles of thermodynamics for my high school graduation. Additionally, I was among the top 1% of students in my state in the National Standard Examination in Astronomy (NSEA).

I'm excited to share that I have been accepted to IIT Madras, to pursue Mechanical Engineering at the undergraduate level and I will be joining this fall.
